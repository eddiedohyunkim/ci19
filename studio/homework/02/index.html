How to plan your commute on a mobile device!

1

Open a new tab on your browser and open Google Maps.

On your mobile device, open the Google Maps app.

2

Make sure you're signed in to your account.

3

On the left menu, click "Set a home address."

At the bottom, tap commute to set up your home and work address.

4

Type in your home address in the search bar and click "Save" button.

Type in your home address in the search bar and tap "Next" button.

5

Once you are back to the main page, click "Set a work address" on the left menu.

Type in your work address in the search bar and tap "Done" button.

6

Type in your work address in the search bar and click "save" button.

You will get a pop-up screen of asking whether to turn on the Location History option. You can choose either "No thanks" or "Turn on."

7

Now on the left menu, you will see the best recommended route from Google's database!

Now on the commute menu at the bottom will show you the best route from Google's database!

Tip

You can always edit your home or work address on the left menu.
